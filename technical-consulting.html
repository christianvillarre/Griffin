Explore Technical Consulting

Griffin’s Technical Consulting platform is the engineering authority layer of the business—combining independent NDT leadership, procedural rigor, field-aligned program support, and advanced technology guidance to help clients strengthen compliance, improve execution, and make high-consequence decisions with greater confidence.

Technical Consulting is where Griffin’s core authority lives. It brings together the expertise, systems thinking, and program-level support required to guide inspection operations in complex industrial environments. This includes Level III consulting, subject matter expertise, written practice development, qualification structure, training support, and technical program oversight—each designed to improve clarity, defensibility, and consistency across the inspection function.

Within that same consulting framework, advanced inspection technologies serve as a focused specialization rather than a separate service line. Automation, robotics, advanced imaging, and technology program development are positioned as an innovation layer inside the broader technical offering—helping clients evaluate new tools, integrate emerging systems responsibly, and modernize inspection workflows without losing alignment with standards, procedures, or field realities.
